Who We Are

Calgary's Painting & Drywall Professionals

Done Right Painting & Drywalling was founded in Calgary with a straightforward mission: deliver the kind of quality work that doesn't need to be explained — it speaks for itself. From day one, we've built our reputation project by project, client by client, on the principle that craftsmanship matters.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a small residential painting crew to a full-service painting, drywall, and basement development company serving homeowners and builders across the greater Calgary area. But the fundamentals haven't changed: skilled tradespeople, honest pricing, and a commitment to every client's complete satisfaction.

We're a local Calgary company. Our team members live in the communities we serve — in Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, and every corner of the city. We care about the work we do here because it's our home too.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide an estimate?
We typically schedule an on-site visit within 2–3 business days and provide a written quote within 24–48 hours of the assessment.
Do you use premium paints and materials?
Yes. We work with industry-leading brands such as Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore. We'll recommend the right products for your specific project and environment.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Done Right Painting & Drywalling is fully licensed in Alberta, carries general liability insurance, and all crew members are WCB covered.
What is included in a basement development?
Our basement packages include framing, insulation, drywall installation and finishing, and full paint. We can also assist with permit applications and coordinate with other trades as needed.
Will my quote change after I approve it?
No. Our quotes are fixed and transparent. The price you approve is the price you pay — unless you request additional scope changes after work begins.
How do I prepare my home before you arrive?
We'll guide you through any preparation needed before the project starts. Generally, we handle all protection and masking ourselves — just ensure we have clear access to the work areas.
